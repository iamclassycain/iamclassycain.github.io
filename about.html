This is My Story

Inspirational yet arresting.
Powerful and engaging.
Stories & imagery for brands, publications, and people.

My name is Robert Cain, but my friends call me Classy Cain. I am a 20-year-old photographer dual-majoring in Applied Mathematics and Media Studies at Claremont McKenna College. I recently returned to the United States after completing a year abroad in Europe and Asia, specifically in Thessaloniki, Manila, and Prague. I worked as a marketing intern and studied media relations and photography, which honed my creative process and fueled my inspiration for future projects.
